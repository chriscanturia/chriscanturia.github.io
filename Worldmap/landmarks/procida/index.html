Procida, Italy

Procida is one of the smallest islands located along the Gulf of Naples. This island is also known for its beauty and natural splendor with a lovely coastline and landscape that captures the heart of every visitor coming to this place. Though Procida does not have an international acclaim as a popular tourist spot, it is one of the most fascinating islands where the rich and the well-known people have their personal villas. Here you can come across houses in hues of pink and yellow with a contrast of the green color of orchards and gardens.
